FINNIK'S FIRST UNDERCITY MISSION

Captain Kane Carrion always kept a watchful eye on his daughter Finnik. Her being a part of the peace force he commanded was nerve wracking, but that was par for the course when raising someone like her. Following a botched store robbery and a stab wound, Kane had enough ground to redirect her. He entrusted her to the command of Lieutenant Jaxton, lead of undercity affairs. The undercity was more treacherous than the pearly crimes of topside, but he knew she would be safe under her uncle's guidance.
