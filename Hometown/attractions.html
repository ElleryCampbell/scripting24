The Spike

From passed down records, a millennium ago, colossal giants occupied and waged war along the lands that are now known as Opportunity. It is heavily theorized that during that fight among them that one particular giant removed a large spike that originated from the United Peaks and impaled into the ground as an improvised attack, creating a large protrusion through Eberron's crust. Since then, the spike still stands and now covers Free City in a shade.

United Peaks

A mountainous terrain that borders the peninsula that Free City occupies. It is home to the eighth largest mountain in the world, Mt. Lanshire. While mountainous terrains tend to be cold, the United Peaks is built very sporadically with crevices that allowed warmer enclosures. One large said enclosure became home to the United Peaks Bird Sanctuary that was part of the Erhaltung 1026 Act to preserve species before their endangerment. It was overturned in 1032 YK by the Free City advisory board.

Project Image

A famed theater created by Jacqueline in hopes to bring her love for 'proper' theater back from the modern world she originated from. It had many famed plays from that era with revitalized portions of the play apparently she was not as fond of.

War Games Arena

Free City is home to the Free City Silverfish. An arena known as the Melody Dome was built in 1022 YK to participate in the traditional games. After its construction and popularity, an entertainment district was built around it, including Project Image.

Monument of the Storm.

A beheaded brass dragon statue that once existed in the town square of the Undercity. It now lies in ruins and remains one of the only places in the Undercity with any greenery at all, particular flowers that can survive without sunlight. People mistake the dragon as a bronze dragon. This monument was originally built in honour of the brass dragon-kin man that lead the 1029YK uprising. A cause that was thwarted by a some double crossing, and Peacekeepers armed with flamethrowers acting under a plan that would become known as "Morning Storm".
